ABOUT US

Maybe it's all that intense chocolate flavor baked into one perfect square; the wonderfully fragrant aroma, or moist decadent center that seductively lingers on the taste buds. Whatever it is, a fresh-baked brownie is the ultimate indulgence.

We at treats and stuff take your love of the humble brownie to a whole new level. By pairing fine gourmet chocolate with farm-fresh ingredients, and trial and error, we've perfected the art of brownie making thereby elevating it into a gourmet treat - not too cakey, not too fudgey, just right. The fun doesn't end there; we've managed to take our signature brownie and customize it according to your needs - we've got many unique toppings and flavors to choose from and we will continue to experiment in the kitchen to bring you the best flavour combinations.

Our goal is to spread joy and simplify gift giving. Much care is taken in all stages of preparing your unique assortment of brownies. Whether you're ordering for yourself or giving to others, you are assured freshness and unsurpassed taste. Our brownies come in a standard box or a gift box and we cater to parties, weddings and corporate events too. We pride ourselves on our service as much as brownies. Our ovens are running hot constantly and we guarantee that fresh baked goodness is at your doorstep within two hours (for standard orders). We go to great lengths to ensure that nothing stands between you and your brownie.
